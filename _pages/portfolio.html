StoryMate: Personalized Interactive Story-Reading

LLM + EDU
Experience StoryMate

StoryMate is a interactive story-reading tool that uses Large Language Model (LLMs) to provide personalized story reading and interaction for children. Specifically, it has four main features:

👥

Customizable Content

StoryMate provides customizable reading content and modes.

💬

Personalized Interaction

The conversation between children and story characters is child-appropriate, adaptive, and personalized.

👀

Attention-Getting Mechanisms

StoryMate addresses children by name and employs a peer-like chatbot to keep children engaged.

💡

Active Thinking

StoryMate uses RAG-based guided conversation for active thinking.

🛠️ Technologies

Large Language Models Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) Full-Stack Development Educational Technology
